## 题目
In a deep-sea scientific expedition lasting three days per cycle, a deep-sea observation station must operate continuously for a total of $270$ hours.

The observation station is equipped with $7$ high-precision environmental sensors, divided into two groups:
\begin{itemize}
\item Group A (Acoustic Group): sensors $A_1, A_2, A_3, A_4$;
\item Group B (Optical Group): sensors $A_5, A_6, A_7$.
\end{itemize}

\textbf{Operating Rules}
\begin{itemize}
\item \emph{Exclusivity:} Throughout the entire $270$-hour mission period, at any given moment, exactly one sensor is in operation.
\item \emph{Time Constraints:}
  \begin{itemize}
  \item For each sensor in Group A, the total operating time (in hours) must be a positive multiple of $7$.
  \item For each sensor in Group B, the total operating time (in hours) must be an integer \emph{at least} $13$.
  \end{itemize}
\end{itemize}

Assuming that the number of on/off switches for each sensor is unrestricted, and considering only the total cumulative operating time of each sensor over the entire mission, determine how many distinct allocation schemes are possible.


## 解答
\textbf{Solution}

Let the total operating time of sensor $A_i$ be $x_i$ hours $(i=1,2,\dots,7)$. Then
\[
x_1+x_2+\cdots+x_7 = 270,
\]
where
\[
x_i \in 7\mathbb{Z}^+ \ (1\le i\le 4),
\qquad
x_j \in \mathbb{Z},\ x_j\ge 13 \ (5\le j\le 7).
\]

Set
\[
x_i=7a_i \quad (1\le i\le 4), \qquad a_i\in\mathbb{N}^*,
\]
and write the Group B times as
\[
x_5=13+u_5,\quad x_6=13+u_6,\quad x_7=13+u_7,
\qquad u_5,u_6,u_7\in\mathbb{Z}_{\ge 0}.
\]
Substituting into the total-time equation gives
\[
7(a_1+a_2+a_3+a_4) + (13+u_5)+(13+u_6)+(13+u_7)=270,
\]
i.e.
\[
7m + 39 + (u_5+u_6+u_7)=270,
\]
where
\[
m=a_1+a_2+a_3+a_4 \ge 4.
\]
Hence
\[
u_5+u_6+u_7 = 231 - 7m.
\]
For feasibility we need $231-7m\ge 0$, so
\[
4\le m \le 33.
\]

\textbf{Counting for a fixed $m$.}

\emph{(i) Group A.} The number of positive integer solutions to
\[
a_1+a_2+a_3+a_4=m
\]
is, by stars and bars,
\[
\binom{m-1}{3}.
\]

\emph{(ii) Group B.} The number of nonnegative integer solutions to
\[
u_5+u_6+u_7 = 231-7m
\]
is
\[
\binom{(231-7m)+3-1}{3-1}=\binom{233-7m}{2}.
\]

Therefore, for each $m$ the number of allocations is
\[
\binom{m-1}{3}\binom{233-7m}{2},
\]
and the total number of distinct allocation schemes is
\[
\sum_{m=4}^{33}\binom{m-1}{3}\binom{233-7m}{2}.
\]

Evaluating this sum yields
\[
\sum_{m=4}^{33}\binom{m-1}{3}\binom{233-7m}{2} \;=\; 62618512.
\]

\bigskip

\textbf{Answer}

\[
\boxed{62618512}
\]
distinct time allocation schemes.

## 答案
62618512

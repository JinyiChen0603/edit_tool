## 题目
In a collectible card game, a deck includes 32 cards: 10 red, 10 yellow, and 10 blue cards numbered 1 to 10, plus two wild cards numbered 0. A player draws a hand of cards, and the total power level is determined by scoring each card numbered $k$ as $2^k$ points. If the total power level is 2004, the hand is considered "optimal." Determine the number of optimal hands.

## 解答
## 解析
用$a_n$表示分值之和等于$n$的牌组数目.于是$a_n$的母函数为
$$
f(x)=\sum_{n=0}^{\infty}a_nx^n=(1+x^{2^0})^2(1+x^{2^1})^3(1+x^{2^2})^3\cdots(1+x^{2^{10}})^3
$$
$$
=\frac{1}{1+x}\left\{(1+x^{2^0})(1+x^{2^1})(1+x^{2^2})\cdots(1+x^{2^{10}})\right\}^3
$$
$$
=\frac{1}{(1+x)(1-x)^3}\cdot(1-x^{2^{11}})^3.
$$
因为$2004<2^{11}$，故$a_{2004}$等于$\frac{1}{(1+x)(1-x)^3}$展开式中$x^{2004}$的系数.而
$$
\frac{1}{(1+x)(1-x)^3}=\frac{1}{(1-x^2)(1-x)^2}=\sum_{i=0}^{\infty}x^{2i}\sum_{j=0}^{\infty}C_{j+1}^1x^j,
$$
故其中$x^{2004}$系数为
$$
a_{2004}=C_{2005}^1+C_{2003}^1+\cdots+C_3^1+C_1^1
$$
$$
=2005+2003+\cdots+3+1=1003^2=1006009.
$$

## 答案
$$1006009$$
